How to get Tier Tested

To get tier tested, join the Discord server & visit the #request-test channel in which you will be able to find a message to verify your account.

Once you verify your account you are required to fill up a form which, once done, will assign you a waitlist role.

Whenever there are tier testers available in your region, you will be able to join.

When you reach #1 in the queue, you will gain access to a private channel to schedule with your tier tester.

Happy Testing!
